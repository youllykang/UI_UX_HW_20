Uniqgift

An app that makes thoughtful gifting easier.

About the Project

Goal: Create a brand-new mobile application that solves a real, clearly identified need in users' everyday lives.

Type: Conceptual

Duration: 3 weeks

Role: UX Designer

Skills: Figma, Invision, Canva

Our Process

This project, Uniqgift, was created in a collaborative effort of three UX designers: Aishwarya, Aivory, and myself. As a class assignment, we were tasked to research, prototype, test, and validate an idea for a brand-new mobile application.

Our process began with brainstorming ideas for an app that would solve a real, clearly identified need in user’s everyday lives. After becoming curious about the stress people often experience when they need to think of a gift for someone, we began our research to better understand people’s gift-search processes to identify what problems exist. Then, we moved onto designing solutions. Throughout this process, we tested and iterated based on the feedback we received to get to the final prototype.
